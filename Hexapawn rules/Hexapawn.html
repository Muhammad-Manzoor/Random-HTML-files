How to play

Hexapawn is based on chess.
Each player has 3 pawns on a board with 9 squares(3x3 arrangement).
The pieces move like chess pawns too.
They can go forward one space if that space is unoccupied, if it is occupied by the opponent then they can't go forward.
You can however move diagonally, but only to take an opponent's piece.

There are three ways to win:

1. Get a pawn to the other side of the board.
2. Take all of your opponent's pieces.
3. Leave your opponent without a possible move.
